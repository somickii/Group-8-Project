Integritas| Excellentiam| Innovatione

CHIPS, Computer Hardware Information Processing Specialists, is the academic club of the Department of Computer and Information Sciences, Northern Caribbean University. It has a membership that exceeds three hundred students that major or minor in a programme in the department.

Gallery

Mission Statement

CHIPS is mandated to: - Unite students of the Computer and Information Sciences Department, - Promote the spirit of inquiry and dedication, - Keep students in touch with the frontiers of Computer Science, - Support our creative interests and abilities in technological skills, and - Promote scholarship within the association, to make our commitments to God our highest objective.
